FAQ

Common questions.

Is there a cost to the program?

No. There is no fee to participate. Althra is founder-first and designed to remove barriers so you can focus on building.

Do you take equity?

Yes, but we've redesigned our terms to ensure we never act as a "brake" on your growth. Our standard investment is $12,500 for 1% equity w/ a $50,000 follow on. However, we believe the market should have the final say on what your company is worth. The Althra Market-Match: If you graduate and successfully raise an aggregate of $100k or more on a higher valuation cap within 6 months, we will match that higher cap. * The Goal: We want you to capture as much upside as possible. If you prove your value is higher, we won't hold you back with "locked-in" early terms. Follow-on Capital: We also reserve the right to invest an additional $50,000 in follow-on capital for all cohort companies. The Paperwork: We use SAFEs (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) for all investments to keep things fast and founder-friendly.

What level of traction do you expect?

We look for teams beyond the idea/prototype stage. Teams showing early signals of validation such as pilots, revenue, or clear user demand.
