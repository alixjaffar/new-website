The Bar Is High

Venture-scale potential.

Enterprise/Consumer software or Hardware enabled software with the potential of venture scale. Clear wedge with real differentiation.

Willing to relocate to Vancouver.

This is a four-month, in-person program. 30+ hours per week from the Althra space. We provide capital upfront to support relocation and full-time participation.

Real market signals.

Proof that customers care — paid pilots, beta users, LOIs, early revenue, or documented customer discovery. We do not accept idea-only teams.

You will show up.

You’ll be working from the Althra space at least 30 hours per week. Extending runway through contract work is understood.
